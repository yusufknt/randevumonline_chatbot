# Share products with WhatsApp users

Updated: Mar 3, 2026

You have multiple ways to share products with your customers.

## Catalog messages

[Catalog messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/catalog-messages) and [Catalog template messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/catalog-template-messages) display a product thumbnail header image, custom body text, and a **View catalog** button. When a customer taps the button, your product catalog appears within WhatsApp.

## Catalog link messages

Use a [Catalog link messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/catalog-link-messages) to send a link to your entire product catalog by assembling a wa.me link and including it in a standard [text message](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/messages/send-messages#text-messages).

## Checkout button messages

[Checkout button template messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/payments/payments-in/checkout-button-templates) allow India-based businesses to showcase one or more products that WhatsApp users in India (with India country calling codes) can purchase without leaving the WhatsApp client.

## Single-product messages

[Single-product messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/single-product-messages) and [Single-product template messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/spm-template-messages) present a single product from your catalog, accompanied by a product image, product title, and product price, along with customizable body text, optional footer text, and an interactive **View** button.

![Single-product message template example](https://scontent.fyei5-1.fna.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/456334558_1165670014537761_4619872146080155046_n.png?_nc_cat=107&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=F_fVljb_McsQ7kNvwFNOIwm&_nc_oc=AdqBjqQy4T6qg4NDuAH8MPjHaF6JBkCx09tQvZqJYYH2FXVfF_mgMuoacP6zH6ym7Ug&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent.fyei5-1.fna&_nc_gid=sQ6_T7ubFlaKrfqhEthYtg&_nc_ss=7b20f&oh=00_Af6kDc1lc05sLjoB55OjWrnAa-83nUPY85qJE0Kq7gxCZw&oe=6A1C0A0E)

## Multi-product messages

[Multi-product messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/multi-product-messages) and [Multi-product template messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/mpm-template-messages) display up to 30 products from your catalog, organized into customizable sections.

![Multi-product message template example](https://scontent.fyei5-1.fna.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/345336924_1476472873159435_9050004394387774321_n.png?_nc_cat=105&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=TwzPu0nWHC8Q7kNvwHNcCEC&_nc_oc=AdoFudCkxjn3VzNudjvVCdVywWwCGStRzX-YlsJ5NRafZ1AoivjdqaUBB7j03W-JVQU&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent.fyei5-1.fna&_nc_gid=sQ6_T7ubFlaKrfqhEthYtg&_nc_ss=7b20f&oh=00_Af6l4ShIuW6DkKDkbrw-y1tm_szV1i_ZeLmX5jCtoGZj1Q&oe=6A1BFF9C)

## Product carousel messages

[Product carousel messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/interactive-product-carousel-messages) and [Product carousel template messages](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/catalogs/product-card-carousel-template-messages) allow you to send a single text message accompanied by a set of up to 10 product cards in a horizontally scrollable view.
