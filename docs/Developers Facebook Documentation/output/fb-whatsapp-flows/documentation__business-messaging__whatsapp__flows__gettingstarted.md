# Get Started

Updated: Oct 28, 2025

This section will help you get started with your very first (demo) Flow.

## Prerequisites

1. Set up [WhatsApp Business Platform](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/business-platform) This will give you access to the [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/partner_home/) as part of your WhatsApp Business Account.
2. [Set up the WhatsApp Business Platform Cloud API](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/get-started) Includes setup of Meta App and phone number
3. To be able to send Flows to your customers you will need to: [Verify your business](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/development/release/business-verification)Maintain a [high message quality](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/messages/send-messages#message-quality).
