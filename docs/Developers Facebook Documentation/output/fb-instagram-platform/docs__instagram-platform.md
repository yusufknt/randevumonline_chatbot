# Instagram Platform

The Instagram Platform from Meta provides tools to help your app user's interact with Instagram users.

### Instagram API with Business Login for Instagram

The [Instagram API with Instagram Login](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/instagram-api-with-instagram-login) allows users of your app to access data in their [Instagram Business](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fbusiness.instagram.com%2F&h=AUCxkI7duUzCFlQjUk8BniniNMsTK77k7w1BInCN5JGIVhidctP4FFYpDBNvHx_sEphUZC8KPPJfyzOUmoePgm30RBiqwu8R_8L5EU9iFig5ipZzDelbMol9olyMin5AQK3XO6FNoNr6Iw) and [Instagram Creator](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2F2358103564437429&h=AUB31R3LS7Zxzwlot5BJ9MoCUDcv2yO8mHtnR7fwTvGEfiar-TaWqdAp7IQqVuPQ4AelmKXGRo6YuvdlIalQSr8LHsrOKD-7f8c0ySb-B3nVpCc83MgtwZpsE9ozKUKWlY8ldPB59643FQ) accounts. This API allows your app to send and receive messages from businesses and creators to Instagram users interested in their business or media, get and publish their media, manage and reply to comments on their media, and identify media where they have been @mentioned by other Instagram users. ***Requires an Instagram Business or Creator Account.***

### Instagram API with Facebook Login for Business

The [Instagram API with Facebook Login for Business](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/instagram-api-with-facebook-login) allows users of your app to access data in their [Instagram Business](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fbusiness.instagram.com%2F&h=AUChDnmzSGtaheavuZF_51sccfeYdbz9Tj75hX6ZKBBPZSe0jS-I5kmZzYstgPViud0as33GnPEm_6wCwnEI5BzC0XOsPUKLY2eP9v1VwHbd41520ngzrHX1PRZaMhT2uDTtQBQHhC3x2Q) and [Instagram Creator](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2F2358103564437429&h=AUA16889I-2sk3qyYtsrccdwOGIihuiG5zSG8jkjnZglRA8RRP7L4b8PMASgnj0IWj2w3QotY8oZE7SjY7swyh0wGQf2EyM8uJANaeZOBBOm5u9x3WLJVMQwtg89BL11q6-JFFdptyw_GA) accounts that are linked to a Facebook Page. The API can be used to get and publish their media, manage and reply to comments on their media, identify media where they have been @mentioned by other Instagram users, find hashtagged media, and get basic metadata and metrics about other Instagram Businesses and Creators. ***Requires an Instagram Business or Creator Account linked to a Facbook Page.***

### Instagram Messaging API via Messenger API

[Messenger API support for Instagram](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/messenger-platform/instagram) (also known as Instagram Messaging API in our Developer Policies) allows Instagram professional accounts to use your app to manage their Instagram and Facebook Page messages using one platform. The API can be used to send and receive messages from Instagram and Facebook users. ***Requires an Instagram Business or Creator Account linked to a Facbook Page.***

### Sharing to Stories

With [Sharing to Stories](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram/sharing-to-stories), your app users can share their content as Instagram stories.

### Sharing to Feed

With [Sharing to Feed](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram/sharing-to-feed), your app users can share their content to their Instagram Feed.

### Embedding

[Embed](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram/embedding) Instagram photo and video posts in your websites.
